# Lei's Emoji & Kaomoji Picker

A SillyTavern extension that adds a Discord-style emoji and kaomoji picker button next to the send button.

## Features

- **Emoji Tab**: Comprehensive emoji collection organized by categories (smileys, emotions, gestures, hearts, animals, food, nature, objects, symbols)
- **Kaomoji Tab**: Pre-loaded kaomoji organized by mood
- **Custom Categories**: Create your own kaomoji categories!
- **Search**: Filter emojis and kaomoji by typing
- **Recent**: Tracks your recently used items for quick access
- **Custom Kaomoji**: Add your own kaomoji to any category
- **Delete Mode**: Remove kaomoji you don't want
- **Persistent Storage**: Your custom kaomoji, categories, and preferences are saved

## Usage

1. Click the ğŸ˜Š button next to the send button
2. Switch between **Emoji** and **Kaomoji** tabs
3. Click a category to filter
4. Click any item to insert it into your message
5. Use the search bar to find specific items

### Adding Custom Kaomoji

1. Switch to the **Kaomoji** tab
2. Click **+ Add Kaomoji**
3. Enter your kaomoji and select a category
4. Click **Save**

### Creating Custom Categories

1. Switch to the **Kaomoji** tab
2. Click **+ New Category**
3. Enter a name (lowercase, no spaces - underscores ok)
4. Click **Create**
5. Your new category appears with a dashed border
6. **Right-click** on custom categories to delete them

### Deleting Kaomoji

1. Switch to the **Kaomoji** tab
2. Click **Delete Mode** (it will highlight red)
3. Click any kaomoji to remove it
4. Click **Delete Mode** again to exit
